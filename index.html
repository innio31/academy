FAQ

Common Questions

Do we need a fast internet connection to use the platform? ▾

The school portal works on standard broadband. The CBT module is fully offline — students sit their exams without internet, and results sync to the portal when connectivity is restored. Parent notifications use SMS as backup when data is unavailable.

Who owns the school data? ▾

Your school owns 100% of its data at all times. You can export all records (students, staff, finance, results) in standard formats at any time. We never share your data with third parties.

Can the system handle multiple arms/classes per year group? ▾

Yes. The platform is built to support any school structure — multiple arms per class (JSS1A, JSS1B, etc.), different subject combinations per class level, and multiple teachers per subject. It scales from small schools to large multi-section institutions.

What happens after the 13-week deployment? ▾

After go-live, you're covered by our annual maintenance plan, which includes bug fixes, security patches, 2 major feature updates per year, and priority support. Our team remains your dedicated technology partner.

Can parents access the system on their phones? ▾

Yes. The parent portal is fully mobile-responsive and works on any smartphone browser — no app download required. Parents can view attendance, results, fee status, and communicate with teachers from their phones.

Is the grading system flexible for our school format? ▾

Absolutely. The report card and grading system is fully configurable — you define the score breakdowns (CA percentages, exam weight), grade boundaries, remarks, and the report card layout. We replicate your existing format exactly.

Do you provide training for our staff and teachers? ▾

Yes, training is included in the deployment. We conduct role-specific sessions: one for administrators, one for teachers, and a guide for parents. Training can be done on-site or virtually, and we provide written documentation for reference.

What payment methods are supported for school fees? ▾

The finance module supports bank transfer reference verification, and can be integrated with Nigerian payment gateways including Paystack and Flutterwave for direct card/bank payments. Remita integration is available for government schools on request.
